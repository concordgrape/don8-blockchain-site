What is Doon8?

Doon8 is a simple blockchain based off of Naivechain (Github). This blockchain stores encrypted records that allow people to anonymously donate to custom fundraisers

How can I donate?

Each fund posted on the site will include a bitcoin address, where people can send their payments. Don8 does not process payments.

How can I access the Github?

All of our source code is open source, feel free to visit it here! Remember to follow the setup instructions!
